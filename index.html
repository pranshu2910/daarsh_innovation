DERMAL INFECTIONS

DAARSH INNOVATIONS has invested substantial efforts into the development of novel solutions for the treatmentsof a range of skin conditions such as Tinea Infections e.g. Tinea Pedis (Athlete Foot), Tinea Cruris (Jock Itch). The first commercial offering “be Clear” is a unique Topical Spray to address Athlete Foot. It is currently undergoing preclinical evaluations.

WOMEN INTIMATE AREA INFECTIONS

DAARSH INNOVATIONS is tirelessly working to develop novel treatments for women intimate area infections caused by bacteria and yeast. The first commercial offering would be “V Clear”, a unique Vaginal Spray to address such infections. It is currently undergoing preclinical evaluations.

CATHETER INFECTIONS

DAARSH INNOVATIONS initiated its journey in the infection control with the development of lock solutions to address catheter related bloodstream infections. Our first commercial offering would be “Stercath” a Catheter Lock Solution. In-vivo studies to prove its efficacy have been successfully completed. Safety studies are in progress.
